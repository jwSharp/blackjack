The Rules of Blackjack

The following are some general rules on how to play the game of Blackjack. To learn more, check out the official rules of Blackjack.

Objective

The goal of the game is to get closer to 21 than your opponent - the dealer.

Player Turn

Each player plays through their hand to completion. The main options for a player is to hit, stand, or split. Check out the definitions of each.

Busting

When a player draws a card that makes their total exceed 21, the player loses. This is known as busting. If the dealer exceeds 21, all of the players who have not busted automatically win.
